Aerial Yoga

Suspended hammock practice

Aerial yoga combines traditional yoga poses with the support of a silk hammock, allowing for deeper stretches, spinal decompression, and fun inversions. The hammock supports your body weight, making challenging poses more accessible while building upper body and core strength.

  • Decompress spine and relieve back pain
  • Build core and upper body strength
  • Improve balance and spatial awareness
  • Experience therapeutic inversions safely

Pilates Postural

Tower · Chair · Spine Corrector · Ladder Barrel

Focused training on specialty Pilates equipment designed specifically for posture correction, spinal alignment, and functional movement. The Tower builds vertical strength against gravity, the Chair challenges balance and unilateral control, the Spine Corrector promotes healthy spinal curves, and the Ladder Barrel enhances flexibility and back extension. Perfect for desk workers, those with postural imbalances, or anyone seeking to move with better alignment.

Focus Areas:
  • Correct forward head posture and rounded shoulders
  • Strengthen posterior chain (back body)
  • Improve hip mobility and pelvic alignment
  • Build functional strength for daily activities
